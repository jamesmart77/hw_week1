About Me

In the beginning of college I had a vague idea of what coding was but certainly lacked an appreciation of its magnitude and power to impact every area of life. Then, one late evening, my mind was rocked with something as simple as VBA. The hook was sunk and I have been pursuing programming ever since.

My enjoyment for programming comes from the design and chase of it all. I like solving problems and creating a great user experience!

When I'm not coding, spending time with my wife (best friend) and our church family is where you will find me. We like getting involved in the community of Somersworth and enjoy going for walks through the woods with our beagle coon hound, York.
